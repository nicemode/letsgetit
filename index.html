Arches National Park

"arches-0" by claytontsmith is licensed under CC BY-NC 2.0

Arches National Park, located near Moab, Utah, is one of the most beautiful places to visit in the U.S.. It is well-known for its eponymous stone arches, some of which measure hundreds of feet long. Historically, it was inhabited by the Ute and Paiute tribes, and petroglyphs can still be found in the area along some of the arches.
